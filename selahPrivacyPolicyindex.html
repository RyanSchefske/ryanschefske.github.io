Selah Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2025

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Selah ("we," "our," "us," or the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our mobile application.

By downloading, installing, or using Selah, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Chat Messages: When you use our AI chat feature, we process the questions and messages you submit to provide responses. These conversations may be stored locally on your device.
  • Bookmarks and Notes: Any verses you bookmark and notes you add are stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences: Your app settings, including preferred Bible translation, font size, and notification preferences.
  • Reading History: Records of chapters and verses you have read, stored locally on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including features accessed, time spent in the App, and interaction patterns.
  • Crash Data: If the App crashes, we may collect diagnostic information to help us improve stability.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, or contact information unless you voluntarily contact our support team.
  • We do NOT collect your location data.
  • We do NOT collect any biometric data.
  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Maintain the App

  • Deliver AI-powered responses to your Bible-related questions
  • Store your reading progress, bookmarks, and preferences locally
  • Send daily verse notifications (if enabled by you)

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve App features and user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs, errors, and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send push notifications you have opted into (daily verses, reading reminders)
  • Respond to your support inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 OpenAI

Our AI chat feature is powered by OpenAI's API. When you submit a question through the chat feature:

  • Your message is transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing
  • OpenAI processes your request to generate a response
  • OpenAI's use of this data is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

Important: We recommend not sharing personally identifiable information, sensitive personal data, or confidential information in your chat messages, as this data is transmitted to third-party servers.

3.2 Apple Services

  • App Store & StoreKit: If you make in-app purchases, transactions are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Push Notifications: We use Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) to deliver notifications. This is governed by Apple's privacy practices.

3.3 Bible API

We use a third-party Bible API to retrieve Scripture text. This service receives requests for specific Bible verses but does not receive any personal information about you.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data is stored locally on your device, including: - Reading history - Bookmarks and notes - Chat session history - App preferences - Cached Bible chapters (for offline access)

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless specifically stated otherwise.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including: - Secure data transmission using HTTPS/TLS encryption - Local data storage using iOS secure storage mechanisms - No collection of unnecessary personal information

4.3 Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the App or clear your data through the App settings
  • Cached Content: Bible chapters are cached locally for up to 30 days
  • Chat History: Stored locally on your device; you can delete it at any time through the App

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control Your Data

You have the right to: - Access: View all your data stored within the App - Delete: Remove your bookmarks, reading history, and chat sessions at any time through Settings > Clear All Data - Opt-Out: Disable notifications at any time through the App settings or your device settings

5.2 Data Portability

Currently, the App does not offer data export functionality. Your data remains stored locally on your device.

5.3 Account Deletion

Since we do not require account creation, there is no account to delete. All your data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by: - Using the "Clear All Data" feature in Settings - Uninstalling the App

6. Children's Privacy

6.1 Age Requirements

Selah is intended for users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) without parental consent.

6.2 Parental Guidance

We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's use of the App, particularly the AI chat feature. The AI provides responses about religious and spiritual topics that parents may wish to discuss with their children.

6.3 COPPA Compliance

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
